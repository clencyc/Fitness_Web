Aerobics Exercises

Aerobic exercise is physical exercise of low
to high intensity that depends primarily on
the aerobic energy-generating process.
"Aerobic" is defined as "relating to,
involving, or requiring oxygen", and refers
to the use of oxygen to meet energy demands during exercise via aerobic metabolism adequately.
